What do I do?

At the moment I live and work in Cologne, but I love to work remotely and am looking for new challenges: Give me bad conversion rates, dropouts, boring texts, and unsatisfied customers. Hire me to optimize your website, shop, app, or service in German and English.

My heart belongs to creating satisfactory customer stories - with words. I have experience in writing copy of various lengths for different target groups, customers, and requirements. My specialties are Microcopy and telling stories: Sometimes the center of attention is just one word, next time I lay out a whole story for you.

I design digital concepts for websites, apps, and interfaces as well as analog concepts for brand experience and corporate identity.

The focus of my texts and concepts is always on the optimal user experience - from conception and planning to storytelling and the creation of pleasing, unique, and consistent content in text and visuals.

Why do you need an optimized copy?

I like to imagine the copy of your website as a person’s voice - a really skilled person’s voice - your best customer advisor, your employee of the year. She welcomes customers at the entrance, guides them through the website, provides advice, and anticipates the customer’s questions so that they can be answered immediately. As a real sales talent, she knows a lot about psychology, so she can even predict your customer’s wishes and control their thoughts and actions. She found her voice and with skill, persuasion, and gentle pressure in the right places, she brings the deal to a close.

My job is to find this voice, define the tonality, and place the words and sentences exactly where they have the greatest possible effect.
